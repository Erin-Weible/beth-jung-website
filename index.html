About Beth

Beth Jung lives in St. Louis, Missouri. She entered political organizing in 2013 and has worked on a variety of state & local campaigns, including statewide ballot initiatives in Arizona and Nevada.

Beth believes that voting and communication with elected officials are the most important tools we have as citizens, and uses her skills and experience to help others be part of the process. In her spare time, Beth loves to read as much as she can, and enjoys attending concerts.
